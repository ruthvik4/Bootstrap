Best Telugu Movies

Jersey

The storyline follows Arjun (Nani), a talented but failed cricketer who decides to return to cricket in his mid-30s, driven by the desire to represent the Indian cricket team, and fulfill his son's wish for a jersey as a gift. Principal photography of the film commenced on 18 October 2018 and was completed in March 2019.

Geetha Govindam

Geetha Govindam is a 2018 Indian Telugu language romantic comedy film written and directed by Parasuram, produced by Bunny Vasu under the banner GA2 Pictures. The film stars Vijay Devarakonda and Rashmika Mandanna, while Subbaraju, Rahul Ramakrishna, and Nagendra Babu play supporting roles.

Bahubali 2

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion is a Indian epic action film.The second cinematic part in the Baahubali franchise. The film follows the sibling rivalry between Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva; the latter conspires against the former and has him killed by Kattappa. Years later, Amarendra's son returns to avenge his death.

Rangasthalam

Chittibabu is a partially deaf, ill-tempered, jovial resident of a village named Rangasthalam. He earns a living by watering irrigational fields using a motor owned by his friend Kolli Rangamma, whom he refers to as atta ,Chittibabu lives with his parents and sister, a farmer who is initially unaware of his hearing impairment.

Maharshi

The plot follows Rishi (Babu), who becomes the CEO of a US-based software company. When his attention is drawn towards the plight of downtrodden farmers by his friend Ravi (Naresh), In a press meet, Rishi makes a speech emphasizing the importance of agriculture and the losses faced due to the farmers' suicides. he sets out to help them.

Arjun Reddy

Arjun Reddy was partially inspired by Sandeep Vanga's life as a physiotherapy student. He worked on the script for two years and it took four to five years for the film to materialise. Shooting Started on 20 June 2016 at Hyderabad and took 86 working days to complete. Other filming locations include some Indian places and Italy.
